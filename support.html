Common Questions about DamaKita
DamaKita is a WebApp that provides Sentiment Analysis to the user, making them feel that they are not alone, and that there is someone out there that can understand their feelings. It is also done by providing Guide and Tips, and Laws, for them to be educated.
DamaKita analyze sentiment of the user by simply chatting, a ChatBot , named "DaKi", will reach out to you.
You also can request to chat with someone.
Click "Learn More" on home page
DamaKita support English, and Filipino.
DamaKita supplies with Educational Articles, How to Do's, Guide and Tips, Contacts, and ability to browse Laws.
We are continously working hard to add features to the site, and constant update to the materials we provide.
